Recruitment Process
STEP ONE: MEET US ON CAMPUS
Ready to create positive change in the world? Good. Let’s get started. We want to make our recruitment process easy, educational and inspiring. First, check with your career services center and student-run organizations for a listing of dates when Morgan Stanley representatives will be at your school. Make sure to attend our campus events, network with as many of our employees as possible and speak to them about their jobs.
STEP TWO: FIND THE PROGRAM FOR YOU
While the majority of our internships and full-time analyst roles are for junior and senior students, some business areas accept rising sophomore and rising junior students for educational programs and employment. You can check the specific job postings for additional information.
STEP THREE: APPLY ONLINE
To apply for a program, submit your resume and cover letter to our careers website. If your school's career website also lists the Morgan Stanley job posting—apply to both postings.
STEP FOUR: ENJOY YOUR INTERVIEW
If your application is successful, first-round interviews may be conducted by phone, on-campus or in person. We hold final interviews at our offices. Some divisions may hold a meeting at an assessment center for technical interviews, group activities, and informational sessions. It’s our goal to match the right person with the right job. At Morgan Stanley, it’s our employees who create our future.
