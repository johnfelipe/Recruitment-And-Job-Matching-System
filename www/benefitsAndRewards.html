Why choose JPMorgan?
Make a real impression and we'll work with you to achieve your career best. The sheer sense of opportunity, scope and pace of change are making Santander one of the best banks in the world to work for. At the same time, we're committed to developing a culture of recognition that encourages turning ideas into action, provides mutual support, and strives for continual improvement. And that commitment hasn’t gone unnoticed – we were recently placed on the Sunday Times ‘25 Best Big Companies to Work For’ and ‘Top 50 Companies For Women’ lists. The votes came from the people who really count – our own colleagues – so it’s a big win for us, which shows our efforts are really paying off.
With us, you’ll enjoy a competitive salary and participate in bonus or incentive schemes, as well as access to a wide range of employee benefits, including:
A minimum of 25 days’ paid holiday (full time equivalent), plus the option to buy/sell holiday Company pension and life cover Sharesave and Partnership Shares Special rates on our financial products and services Discounts on health and lifestyle products Access to company cars Using our online benefits portal, santanderisyou, you’ll be able to build a rewards package that suits your unique needs, dependent on your level and role.

Employee assistance
Our Employee Assistance Programme (EAP) is an independent telephone helpline available to colleagues and immediate members of their family. The EAP provides confidential support, guidance and advice to help resolve a wide range of issues – both professionally and personally.
Flexible work options
We're committed to supporting our people in finding a healthy work-life balance. That’s why we offer a range of flexible work options including part-time, job share, career breaks and flexible holiday options.
Learning and Development
Learning and development in Santander is as important to us as it is to you. Just as our customers expect a first-class service you can expect learning and development opportunities that are first-class; providing the support to manage your career development and achieve your own personal ambitions.
We approach your learning journey as a partnership. Developing your skills and knowledge is going to be critical to your success, and we'll ensure you learn to excel in looking after our customers. That might mean attending a course, using an online package or being coached by an experienced member of staff.
We're strictly governed by policies and laws, so for certain roles such as those that involve giving advice on products, we make sure everyone is up to speed. Working with some of the best training experts in the fields, you’ll undergo formal training – both in the classroom, and in a live environment – and you'll need to pass tests to prove you know your stuff. We're serious about getting it right, so we’re looking for people who take learning seriously.
Once you're settled in, we'll want you to think about your future and how you drive your personal development. It helps to think ahead and ensure that your career is moving in the right direction. We'll support you by providing access to a wide range of training opportunities and learning materials to help you learn new skills. Most importantly, it’s all about giving you room to grow – speaking to you directly, finding out your ambitions and setting out a clear path – with objectives, areas for development and real opportunities to get there.
